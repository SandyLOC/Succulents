Echeveria is a family of rose-shaped succulents native to the semi-desert regions of Central America. Because of its charming leaves' structure, Echeverias are one of the most well-known succulents.

There are plenty of types with different gorgeous colors when it comes to Echeverias. Same like other succulents, they are super easy to grow and take care of.
